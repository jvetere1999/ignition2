## Review (Spaced Repetition)

Flashcard system using SM-2 algorithm.

### How It Works
1. Cards are shown at optimal intervals
2. Rate your recall (1-5)
3. Cards reschedule based on performance
4. Difficult cards shown more often

### Rating Scale
- **1** - Didn't remember at all
- **2** - Barely remembered
- **3** - Remembered with difficulty
- **4** - Remembered well
- **5** - Perfect recall

### Creating Cards
1. Go to **Review**
2. Click "Add Card"
3. Enter front (question) and back (answer)
4. Save

### Rewards
- 15 XP per review session
- 5 coins per session
- Progress toward review quests
