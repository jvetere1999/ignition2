# Training Programs

Multi-week training plans that bridge Exercise and Planner.

## What Are Programs?

Programs are structured training plans:
- Span multiple weeks (4-16 typically)
- Contain scheduled workouts
- Include deload weeks for recovery
- Auto-schedule to your Planner

## Creating a Program

1. Go to **Exercise** > **Programs** tab
2. Click "New Program"
3. Configure:
   - **Name** - e.g., "12-Week Strength Builder"
   - **Description** - Goals and overview
   - **Duration** - Number of weeks
   - **Goal** - Strength, Hypertrophy, Endurance, Weight Loss
   - **Difficulty** - Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced
   - **Deload Weeks** - Select weeks for reduced intensity

## Building Weeks

Each week contains workout slots:

1. Click on a week to expand
2. Add workouts to specific days:
   - Select from your saved workouts
   - Assign to a day (Monday-Sunday)
3. For deload weeks:
   - System reduces intensity automatically
   - Workouts marked with "Deload" indicator

## Starting a Program

1. Review your program structure
2. Click "Start Program"
3. Choose whether to schedule to Planner
4. Workouts appear on your calendar

When scheduled:
- Workouts added as calendar events
- Notifications before each workout
- Mark complete from Planner or Exercise

## Deload Weeks

Deload weeks feature:
- Reduced intensity (60-80% normal)
- Same exercises, lighter weights
- Essential for recovery and growth

Typically placed:
- Every 4th week
- Before a testing week
- After high-intensity blocks

## Progression Rules

Programs can include progression:
- Weekly weight increases
- Rep range changes
- Volume modifications

## Completing a Program

After the final week:
1. Program marked as complete
2. Bonus XP reward
3. Achievement unlocked

## Tips

1. **Be realistic** - Match program to your schedule
2. **Plan recovery** - Include deload weeks
3. **Track progress** - Review PRs during program
4. **Adjust as needed** - Programs are flexible
