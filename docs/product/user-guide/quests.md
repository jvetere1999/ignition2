# Quests

Quests are challenges that reward XP and coins.

## Types of Quests

### Daily Quests
Reset every day at midnight:
- "Complete 2 focus sessions"
- "Log a workout"
- "Review 10 flashcards"

### Weekly Quests
Reset every Monday:
- "Complete 10 focus sessions"
- "Log 3 workouts"
- "Finish a lesson"

### Special Quests
Limited-time challenges with bonus rewards.

## Viewing Quests

The Quests page shows:
- Active quests with progress bars
- Rewards (XP and coins)
- Time remaining (for timed quests)

## Automatic Progress

Quests update automatically when you:
- Complete a focus session
- Log a workout
- Finish a review
- Complete a habit

No need to manually mark progress!

## Rewards

Each quest shows its rewards:
- **XP** - Experience points for leveling
- **Coins** - Currency for Market

Harder quests offer higher rewards.

## Quest Categories

Quests are linked to activities:
- **Focus** - Productivity quests
- **Exercise** - Workout quests
- **Learning** - Educational quests
- **Habit** - Consistency quests

## Universal Quests

Admins create quests available to all users:
- Same challenges for everyone
- Updated regularly
- Seasonal and themed quests

## Tips

1. **Check daily** - Don't miss daily quest resets
2. **Stack activities** - One action can progress multiple quests
3. **Prioritize** - Focus on quests close to completion
4. **Plan ahead** - Weekly quests need consistent progress
