# Getting Started

Welcome to Passion OS! This guide will walk you through your first steps.

## Creating an Account

1. Visit [passion-os.ecent.online](https://passion-os.ecent.online)
2. Click "Sign In"
3. Choose your authentication provider:
   - **Google** - Sign in with your Google account
   - **Microsoft** - Sign in with your Microsoft/Azure account

## Age Verification

Passion OS requires users to be 16 years or older. You'll be asked to confirm your age during first sign-in.

## Account Approval

New accounts require admin approval. After signing in:
1. You'll see the "Pending Approval" page
2. Wait for an admin to approve your account
3. You'll be redirected to the app once approved

## First Steps

Once approved, you'll land on the **Today** dashboard. Here's what to do first:

### 1. Set Up Your Habits

Go to **Habits** and create your daily habits:
- "Complete a focus session"
- "Workout for 30 minutes"
- "Review flashcards"

### 2. Create Your First Quest

Quests are challenges that earn XP and coins. Browse available quests in the **Quests** tab.

### 3. Start a Focus Session

Try the **Focus** timer:
1. Click "Start Focus"
2. Work for 25 minutes
3. Take a 5-minute break
4. Repeat!

### 4. Explore Production Tools

If you're a music producer, check out:
- **Shortcuts** - DAW keyboard shortcuts
- **Arrange** - Piano roll and drum machine
- **Reference** - Audio reference library

## Mobile Access

Install as a PWA (Progressive Web App):
1. Visit the app on your phone's browser
2. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
3. Access the mobile-optimized version at `/m`
