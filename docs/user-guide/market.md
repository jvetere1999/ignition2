# Market

Redeem earned coins for personal rewards.

## Accessing Market

Go to **Market** from the sidebar or Today dashboard.

## Reward Types

### Personal Rewards
Custom rewards you create:
- "Order takeout"
- "Buy a new game"
- "Take a day off"
- "Watch a movie"

### Streak Shields
Protect your streaks:
- Prevents streak reset for one day
- Consumed automatically when needed
- Stock up for peace of mind

### Coming Soon
- XP boosters
- Theme unlocks
- Badge customizations

## Creating Rewards

1. Click "Add Reward"
2. Set details:
   - **Name** - Reward description
   - **Cost** - Coins required
   - **Category** - Type of reward
3. Click "Create"

## Purchasing

1. Browse available rewards
2. Check your coin balance
3. Click "Purchase"
4. Reward is redeemed

## Coin Balance

Your coin balance is shown:
- In the Market header
- On the Progress page
- In the Today dashboard

## Tips

1. **Set meaningful rewards** - Things you actually want
2. **Balance costs** - Mix cheap and expensive
3. **Don't hoard** - Spend coins to stay motivated
4. **Stock shields** - Keep 2-3 streak shields
