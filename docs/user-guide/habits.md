# Habits

Habits help you build consistent daily routines while earning rewards.

## What Are Habits?

Habits are repeatable daily tasks that:
- Can be checked off each day
- Reward XP and coins on completion
- Build streaks for consistency
- Connect to quests for bonus rewards

## Creating a Habit

1. Go to **Habits**
2. Click "+ New Habit"
3. Use a preset or create custom:
   - **Title** - What you'll do
   - **Category** - Focus, Exercise, Learning, Journal, General
   - **XP Reward** - How much XP to earn (default: 10)
   - **Coin Reward** - How many coins to earn (default: 5)
4. Click "Create Habit"

## Preset Habits

Quick-add common habits:
- Complete a focus session
- Workout for 30 minutes
- Review 10 flashcards
- Write in journal
- Produce for 20 minutes
- Practice an instrument

## Completing Habits

1. View your habits list
2. Click the circle next to a habit
3. Watch it mark as complete
4. XP and coins are awarded automatically

## Streaks

Maintaining daily habits builds streaks:
- **Current Streak** - Consecutive days completed
- **Longest Streak** - Your best streak ever

### Protecting Streaks

Purchase a **Streak Shield** from the Market to protect against:
- Missed days resetting your streak
- Life interruptions

## Categories

Organize habits by category:
- **Focus** - Productivity tasks (orange)
- **Exercise** - Physical activity (green)
- **Learning** - Educational tasks (blue)
- **Journal** - Reflection and writing (purple)
- **General** - Everything else (gray)

## Progress Tracking

The progress bar shows:
- Habits completed today
- Total habits to complete
- Percentage complete

## Tips

1. **Start small** - Begin with 2-3 habits
2. **Be specific** - "Read for 20 minutes" vs "Read more"
3. **Stack habits** - Link new habits to existing routines
4. **Review weekly** - Adjust habits that aren't working
